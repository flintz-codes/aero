Inspection and Related Investigative/Corrective Actions
(g) Within 60 months after the effective date of this AD: Inspect the applicable motor-operated
valves (MOVs) to determine whether an MOV with the affected part number identified in the
Accomplishment Instructions of the applicable service bulletin is installed. A review of airplane
maintenance records is acceptable in lieu of this inspection if the part number of the part can be
conclusively determined from that review. Do all applicable related investigative and corrective
actions before further flight. Do all actions in accordance with the Accomplishment Instructions of
the applicable service bulletin.
